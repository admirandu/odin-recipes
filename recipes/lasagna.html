Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish amde with layers of tender pasta sheetss, rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel, and melted cheese baked to golden perfection. It's hearty, flavourful, and perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce (Ragu)

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 2 celery sticks, finely diced
  • 500g minced beef
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 150ml beef stock
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Bechamel Sauce

  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 600ml milk
  • A pinch of grated nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Layers

  • 250g dried or fresh lasagna sheets
  • 100g grated mozzarella
  • 40g grated Parmesan

Steps

Meat Sauce

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan or saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, carrots, and celery. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionalyy, until softened.
  3. Stir in the garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the minced beef and cook until browned all over, breaking up any lumps with a wooden spoon.
  5. Stir in the tomato puree, then add the chopped tomatoes, beef stock and oregano
  6. Season with salt and pepper, bring to the boil, then reduce the heat and simmer gently for 25-30 minutes, until thickened and rich.

Bechamel Sauce

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over a medium heat.
  2. Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute to form a smooth paste.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk, a little at a time, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens to a smooth, creamy consistency.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and a little nutmeg if you like.

Lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C (180C fan)/Gas Mark 6
  2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce over the base of an ovenproof dish.
  3. Cover with a layer of lasagna sheets, then a layer fo bechamel sauce.
  4. Repeat the layers (meat, pasta, bechamel) until all the sauces are used up, finishing with a layer of bechamel on top.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan evenly over the surface.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, until bubbling and golden on top.
  7. Allow the lasagna to rest for 5-10 minutes before serving - this helps it set nicely for neat slices.
