Shepherd's Pie

Home

Description

Shepherd's Pie is a traditional British dish made with minced lamb cooked in a rich gravy with onions, carrots, and peas, topped with creamy mashed potatoes and baked until golden and crisp on top. It's heary, warming, and perfect for a cosy family meal.

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 500g minced lamb
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 250ml lamb or beef stock
  • 100g frozen peas
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Mashed Potato Topping

  • 900g potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 50g butter
  • 100ml milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

Filling

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan or saucepan over mediumm heat.
  2. Add the onion and carrots, and cook for about 5 minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Stir in the garlic and cook for 1 minute more.
  4. Add the minced lamb and cook until browned all over, breaking it up with a wooden spoon.
  5. Stir in the tomato puree, Worcestershire sauce, and thyme.
  6. Pour in the stock, bring to the boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes until thickened.
  7. Stir in the peas, season well with salt and pepper, and remove from the heat.

Mashed Potatoes

  1. Boil the potatoes in salted water for about 15 mintues until tender.
  2. Drain well and return to the pan.
  3. Add the butter and milk, then mash until smooth and creamy.
  4. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Shepherd's Pie

  1. Preheat the oven to 200*C/Gas Mark 6.
  2. Spoon the lamb filling into a large ovenproof dish.
  3. Spread the mashed potato evenly over the top, starting at the edges to seal in the filling.
  4. Use a fork to create a decorative pattern on the surface.
  5. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese for a golden crust.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until bubbling and lightly browned on top.
